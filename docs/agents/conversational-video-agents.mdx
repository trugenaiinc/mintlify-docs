Conversational Video Agents are the core of Trugen’s real-time interactive experience layer-empowering AI systems to communicate as naturally and expressively as humans.
Built on advanced speech understanding, visual perception, and contextual intelligence, these agents **see, listen, understand, and respond instantly** with lifelike avatars that convey emotion and intent.

**sub-second responses, natural turn-taking, and emotionally intelligent communication.**

## Key Features

 - Real-time **speech recognition and expressive voice synthesis**
 - **Vision perception** with gesture and gaze awareness
 - **Contextual reasoning** through advanced language models
 - **Knowledge grounding** using your own content, documents, APIs, or data
 - **Tool and function calling** to perform realtime operations or fetch live information
 - **Customizable persona templates** for personality, tone, and conversational behavior
 - **Lifecycle & speech event callbacks** for analytics and workflow automation
 - Simple **frontend integration** via iFrame or SDK

## Why Trugen Conversational Agents?

Trugen agents are engineered for **high-speed, high-fidelity, multi-turn conversation** driven by multimodal understanding.
Unlike traditional chatbots, they:

- **Speak and react with authentic facial motion and expression**
- **See and understand** gestures, engagement, and body movement
- Handle **interruptions, backchanneling, and natural pacing**
- Adapt responses based on **context, intent, and environment**
- Can interact **hands-free**, enhancing accessibility and user comfort

Whether you’re building a next-gen assistant, avatar coach, AI interviewer, or automated sales presenter, Trugen provides the foundation to create compelling, human-level digital experiences-in minutes.
